Katari-Wolf

  Katari-Wolf Technology LLC was founded in March 2021 by Kyle Ward, a Computer Science major at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Katari-Wolf is a technology design and research company that does not specialize in any one field of science or engineering. The idea behind Katari-Wolf is not only to develop cutting-edge technology that will push humanity into the future, but more importantly to teach and inspire. There is a reason we are a private company and will never answer to a board of stuffy executives in suits. It's because we want our company to be driven by our employees and our clients. When it comes to deciding what project or direction for KW to go to next, that decision is made entirely by the employees, based upon what everyone is most interested in. This allows us as a company to always stay fresh, feel new, and continue to learn! It also means that our products are always at peak quality regardless of what it is, because everyone working on it is passionate, excited, driven and ready to change the world! Our primary goal is to design and develop technology that will not only inspire future generations but also to give them the tools they need to continue to inspire and contiue to push the boundaries of what's possible. To push beyond understanding, beyond comprehension. To dare to go where no one has gone before, and redefine the impossible. To forever find joy and unity in the pursuit of knowledge.
